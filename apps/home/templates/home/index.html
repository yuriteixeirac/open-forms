Core features

Security

OpenForms runs entirely within your private infrastructure. No external CDNs, no third‑party APIs, no telemetry — every form submission and uploaded asset stays inside your network, under your control. Scalable, encrypted, and private by design.

Control

You decide who sees what, when data disappears, and how long forms live. OpenForms is built for organisations that take data responsibility seriously — no mysterious defaults, no vendor lock‑in.

Ease-of-use

One Docker command gets you running. Creating a form is as simple as typing a few field labels — no drag‑and‑drop complexity, no training required. Both admins and respondents get a clean, straightforward experience that respects their time and their privacy.
